About me

Hiya! My name is Frank, and I LOVE tech! Most of the time, I let my interests fuel my passion for my work; which ends up taking me down some deep rabbit holes. I’ve put on many hats in the past working from the bottom of the stack developing PCBs, to developming embedded applications and fiddling with the Linux Kernel, to (now) working as a cloud architect developing and administering infrastructure and automation.

Working at different levels of the stack provides peripheral vision in my work, and restrains me from getting fixating on certain aspects of the project(s). Whenever I am free though, I do love a deep-dive into the underlying technologies to understand exactly how things work under the hood.

I am very fond of automation because it elimates toil from my day-to-day. Got something that you have to repeat mechanically? Automate it! Worried that someone will incorrect perform some sequence of actions? Automate it! Want to increase the efficiency of your system (in some scenarios)? Automate it! For every part of job/life you can automate, you free up a little bit more time for yourself to explore other (perhaps more exciting) things. Overtime, I’ve found this to be a highly effective strategy for augmenting my day.

About this site

After a year of procrastion, I’ve finally decided to put everything together and get this site out. I’ve been working on a couple of projects and need a place to record my exploratory process; what better than a blog eh?

My posts should reflect the chronological development of my projects. For now, I will keep things simple and use the tags to organize related topic. Perhaps in the future when there is sufficient content, I will organize them properly into a portfolio…
